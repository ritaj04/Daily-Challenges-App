# Daily-Challenges-App

## Description
 The daily challenges app is an app that empowers you to break down your
 goals into manageable tasks

 ## Features

1. **Custom Challenge Creation:**
   Easily add personalized challenges that align with your specific goals, whether it's fitness, learning, or personal growth.

2. **Progress Tracking:**  
   Monitor your achievements with intuitive visual indicators that show how close you are to completing each challenge.

3. **Daily Reminders:**  
   Set up notifications to keep you focused and motivated, ensuring you stay on track to reach your goals.
